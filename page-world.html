The world of signal pending takes place in an anthropomorphized Philippines; the same old bustling and diverse archipelago but its citizens are local fauna in real life and fables.
A literal salad bowl as it is home to a wide range of animal friends, separated in tiers of three barangays: brgy. asanorya, patani, and brgy. kintsay, crowned and watched over by a cell tower; the main setting of SIGNAL PENDING, the home island of MAX, RAMO, EGUL, and NOKMA.
Past the stratosphere and within the milky way floats the ever churning satellite that holds the world together via internet connection.
